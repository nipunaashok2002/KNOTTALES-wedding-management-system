About KnotTales

We are a passionate team of three at KnotTales, united by one mission — to make Indian wedding planning effortless, inspiring, and truly personal. With diverse strengths and a shared love for creativity and technology, we help couples discover the perfect vendors, explore unique inspirations, and find the support they need to craft unforgettable celebrations. From curated vendor matches to beautifully designed wedding websites, our goal is to simplify every step of the journey while preserving the magic, culture, and emotion that make Indian weddings extraordinary. Together, we are building more than a platform we are creating a space where stories begin, memories are designed, and every couples tale is woven with joy.
